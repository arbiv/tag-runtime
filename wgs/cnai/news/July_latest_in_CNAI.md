## Key Developments

### Demos and presentations on

* Event driven approach to enhance responsiveness of RAG systems orchestrated on Kubernetes by Janakiram MSV [here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-sWl4XBRsFs)

* Calum Murray and Matthias Wessendorf demonstrated a proof-of-concept using Knative EventTypes ([code](https://github.com/keventmesh/llm-tool-provider) | [blog](https://knative.dev/blog/articles/llm-agents-overview/)).

* [KAITO](https://github.com/Azure/kaito) project discussions and sandbox applicaiton submission (presented by Bangqi Zhu)

### Sustainability Initiative

A proposal to start collaborating on a white paper for AI and Sustainability, details [here](https://github.com/cncf/tag-runtime/issues/168#issue-2405770168).

### CNAI Landscape Update

In case you missed it, the CNCF AI landscape is shown as a separate group on the CNCF landscape. View it [here](https://landscape.cncf.io/?group=cnai).
